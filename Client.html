Momentum is a utility mod for Minecraft anarchy servers. The client is public (but not open source) and will be updated as frequently as possible.

Momentum module list

CHAT:

[CHATSUFFIX - adds a suffix at the end of your messages], [CHATPREFIX - adds a prefix to the beginning of your messages], [SPAMMER - spams messages in chat]

COMBAT:

[CRYSTALAURA - automatically crystals nearby players], [AUTOTOTEM - automatically replaces totems in your offhand], [FASTBOW - makes the release time for bows almost non-existant], [QUICKEXP - automatically mends your armor]

GUI:

[ACTIVEMODULES - shows the modules you have toggled on the hud], [WATERMARK - shows the client name and version on the hud], [HUD - displays various information on the game overlay]

MISC:

[ANTIHUNGER - makes it so you lose no hunger], [DISCORDRPC - shows a custom discord rich presence on your discord profile], [PLAYERALERT - notifies you when a player is within render distance of you]

MOVEMENT:

[BIOMESPEED - makes you move faster in water and ice], [SPRINT - automatically sprints for you], [TIMER - changes the world ticks to a custom value], [VELOCITY - changes player velocity attributes]

RENDER

[FULLBRIGHT - makes the world much brighter], [NOWEATHER - disables client side weather], [WORLDCOLOR - changes the color of the fog and sky]
